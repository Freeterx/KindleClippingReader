**Kindle Clippings Organizer — Chrome Extension**

A **Chrome-based extension** that lets you easily **import, browse, and organize your Kindle clippings**—including **highlights, notes, and bookmarks**—directly in your browser. **No installation or setup required**: simply open the extension, load your `My Clippings.txt` file, and instantly view your reading insights in a clean, searchable interface.

Quickly **export selected highlights to OneNote or copy them to the clipboard**, making it easy to review, share, or reuse your best ideas.

**Simple. Fast. Built for Kindle readers.** ✨
